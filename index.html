About

Welcome to The Queer Book Nook: your go-to destination for insightful and inclusive reviews that celebrate the vibrant world of LGBTQ+ representation in literature. This site has been created to celebrate the diverse voices, experiences and narratives that queer literature brings, especially when created by queer individuals themselves.

Whether you're a seasoned reader or just beginning your journey into queer literature, these reviews aim to analyse books with a queer lens, guiding you towards captivating stories from authors new and old to the writing career. Fundamentally, these reviews take literature that promotes itself to the LGBT+ community specifically and identifies how well the inclusion of the queer community is managed.

Join us on a literary adventure to identify literature that truly reflects the authentic queer experience.
